Cat Lesson #1: Cats are Magical

All cats from this dimension have magic abilities, unique skills, and an instictual sense of danger.

Cat Lesson #2: Cat Skills

All cats have skills! Mine are Telepathy and Telekinesis, but every cat has their own. The skills can range from anything from Fire Starting to Energy Shields! Here are a few of the more common skills.

  • Levitation: Allows the cat to levitate.(This is different from telekinesis, which levitates other objects.)
  • Single Element Blast: Allows the cat to create elemental energy blasts from a single element. The element a cat has will not change, but will always be the same.
  • Enhanced Senses:­ Gives the cat exceptional sight, hearing, smell, taste, and touch.
  • Single Element Shield:­ Allows the cat to create a shield from an element. Like the single element blast
  • Gift of Sight:­ Allows the cat to see small bits of a possible future. This future is not set in stone, and can change due to the actions of those involved.

Cat Lesson #3: Mutations

Some cats have mutations. A mutation is a physiological change to the body of a cat. Mutations may include wings, forked tails, or other changes to the cats body. Not all cats are mutated, but it is only semi-rare to have a mutation. I have a mutation: angel wings!

Cat Lesson #4: Cats are the Best!

All cats are maximal elements. This means that every cat is the best and they cannot be compared to one another.
